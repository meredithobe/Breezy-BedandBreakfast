Welcome to Charming Bed & Breakfast

Located in the heart of Missouri, Breezy Bed & Breakfast offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Our historic home, built in the 1900s, has been lovingly restored to provide our guests with a comfortable and welcoming experience.

Our Mission

At Charming Bed & Breakfast, our mission is to provide a warm, homey atmosphere where guests can relax, recharge, and create lasting memories. We take pride in offering personalized service and a delicious breakfast made with locally sourced ingredients.

Our Rooms

Each of our uniquely decorated rooms has been designed to reflect the charm and character of our home. From cozy retreats to spacious suites, we offer accommodations to suit every traveler's needs. Guests can enjoy amenities such as free Wi-Fi, en-suite bathrooms, and stunning views of the surrounding gardens.

Explore the Area

Situated near numerous hiking trails, local wineries, and quaint shops, our location is perfect for exploring the beauty of the countryside. We are happy to provide recommendations and assist with planning your itinerary to make the most of your stay.

Meet Your Hosts

Your hosts, Meredith and Jonathan, are passionate about hospitality and are dedicated to making your stay unforgettable. With years of experience in the hospitality industry, they look forward to welcoming you to their home.
